Kamu

A fine place to have a coffee in Breda is Kamu. It is a bicycle store on the Boschstraat that also brews delicious coffees. Or is it a coffee bar where their love of bicycles is shared? You can fill it in yourself. In any case, I think it's a great coffee bar/bicycle shop/bakery in Breda.

Yirga

Yirga is an espresso bar in one of the oldest streets in Breda, namely the St. Annastraat. The small, light Yirga has a Scandinavian calm where the openness of the coffee shop is reflected. There is an open kitchen where fresh cakes are baked every day. You can take a seat at the bar while the barista makes your filter coffee. Whether you choose to open your laptop, read newspaper or want to enjoy a cup of coffee with friends, everyone is welcome at Yirga.

Koffiebar Sowieso

They serve the coffees at the bar, with the coffee beans being roasted in Amsterdam itself. For this, the owner Jop drives up and down almost every Monday to roast the fresh beans. And that's always handy, because they know exactly what's at the mill. The coffees are prepared with organic whole milk. Prefer not to use whole milk? No problem, you can also choose soy, oat, almond or coconut milk. Or are you more adventurous? Then order the changing blend and be surprised.

Inspire

Get Inspired! With a view on Breda's Grote Markt you can enjoy the tastiest coffee, fresh tea and fresh fruit juices. Do you want to relax on the couch? It is possible at Inspire! Do you want to surf the internet behind your laptop? It is possible at Inspire! Would you like to get today's news from the newspaper? You can at Inspire! They import their own different beans and use as their House Blend coffee from Caffenation so you can always enjoy the best quality coffee.

Coffeelab

Coffeelab Breda is located near the railway station. We know this coffee bar from Coffeelab Eindhoven. In general terms, this coffee bar is similar to the one in Eindhoven: delicious coffee, green oasis, local products and lots of seats. Whether you want to catch the train with a coffee, or you enjoy your coffee in the bar. Coffeelab makes your coffee with love!

Mr Morris

Mr.Morris is the ultimate mix of a juice, coffee bar and healthy lunchroom in the Dwaalgebied of Tilburg. Their menu includes responsible and healthy fresh juices, unique sandwiches, salads and coffee specialties. They only work with high quality fresh ingredients, mostly organic and fair trade. Moreover, all their products are homemade. An experienced barista prepares your coffee with specialty coffee beans from Lucifer Coffee Roasters. At the same time they prepare your juice and food at the bar within minutes. For a healthy breakfast, lunch, juice or a delicious coffee in Tilburg, Mr.Morris is the place to be.

Stoom013

In 2017, Bob and Lukas opened a coffee shop: Stoom013. It is a special location in Tilburg, namely the Spoorzone. It is a hodgepodge of businesses, creative entrepreneurs and fun new hotspots. It is about a 10 minute walk from Central Station. Hidden behind the Hall of Fame of the Cultuurfabriek, you will find the old train carriage of Stoom013: a coffee bar that is a must in Tilburg! Hometown, The Hague

You can still expect excellent coffee, of course, but there's more! Start your day at Hometown with a full breakfast. Bowls, buttery brioche or banana bread? Or are you more into oatmeal and French toast? They have it all! Their baristas are eager to pour your favorite coffee. Enjoy a slow brew V60 Filter, a Chemex coffee or go for shot Monin syrup. Filtro, The Hague

This coffee bar is located in the historic center of The Hague. Start your morning here with a coffee before you go shopping or take a long walk. They have international coffee roasters on the menu, such as The Barn and Five Elephant from Berlin.

Broei, Utrecht

At BROEI, they work as much as possible with local and small-scale farmers, brewers, roasters and growers. For dairy and more, they work with the Lindenhoff. For vegetables, fruits and herbs with the Lindenhoff and De Groentenakker. The fair trade coffee comes from the guys at KEEN. Their kitchen revolves around vegetables. Lots and lots of vegetables. They are constantly innovating and discovering and creating new dishes (some of which are vegan). And we put our heart and soul into it. Their drinks are as much as possible direct trade, local or from smaller entrepreneurs. they also make a lot ourselves like the kombucha, the Iranian sekanjabin and the iced tea.

Coffeelab, Eindhoven

On September 1, 2020, they opened their doors at Eindhoven Station in the former restaurant. The iconic spot with monumental status is a stone's throw away from where the COFFEELAB adventure started: the Stationsplein. The new start on the second floor of the station felt like coming home. At this location, you can go for your daily cup(s) of coffee, both to go and to stay, for an extensive breakfast and lunch menu, but also for all-day brunch and cocktail boards. The following applies to our range of products: if we can make it ourselves, then we will! From home-made lemonade to our own banana bread.

Borgman & Borgman, Leiden

Borgman Borgman is a coffee shop in Leiden on the Nieuwe Rijn. The love for coffee has been over since 2013 and they have been roasting their own coffee beans ever since. The lovely terrace on the canal invites you to enjoy a cup of coffee with the morning sunshine at this fun coffee shop.
