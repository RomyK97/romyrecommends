Hutspot

Hutspot Breda offers a unique combination of timeless fashion, innovative design and local art. Aiming to curate an accessible platform, they provide young designers and artists an opportunity to sell their goods alongside established brands.

Latte's & Literature

In this bookstore/coffee place you will find all genres, from classic and contemporary literature, suspense, non-fiction, poetry, science fiction, fantasy, children's books, young adult, gift books, travel stories, lonely planets, graphic novels, to comics and manga. In addition to English-language literature, we have a diverse selection in Dutch-language cookbooks and offer the option of ordering Dutch-language books.

Dille & Kamille

At Dille & Kamille you will find everything for home, garden and kitchen. Essential basics and delicious gadgets, to spoil yourself or to give as a gift. The pleasure of beautiful tableware, the ease of kitchen tools that fit well in your hand, the seductive scent of herbal tea, the softness of soap. A special touch to an everyday product. That's what makes life enjoyable.

Swan Breda

Swan Market offers a stage to all those great creatives, who make or design their products with heart and soul. They try to inspire adventurous and conscious shoppers and make them happy with their always innovative products.

J'Adore Vintage

Here you can buy 1 kilo of vintage clothing for only €35! All items are carefully inspected and purchased. So you are always assured of quality. The garments are mainly from France, but also from Italy, Scandinavia and America.

Søstrene Grene

At Søstrene Grene they have a special focus on the natural joy and beauty of the little things in life. In these stores you will always find a wide range of products. Many of them they have designed themselves, inspired by their Scandinavian heritage. they believe that beauty is in the details and therefore always have an eye for the importance of the choice of materials and colors.

Cheap Fashion, Rotterdam

You can spend hours browsing through all kinds of clothing to find your perfect outfit. You can find everything here, from beautiful summer dresses, skirts, sweaters and sneakers to kimonos, party dresses and glitter tops.

Episode, Rotterdam

Another store that you can't miss as a vintage lover is Episode. You can find this vintage store chain in many major cities.

The American Book Centre, Amsterdam

The American Book Center (ABC), an innovative and stubbornly independent English-language book store, has been an institution in Amsterdam for more than 40 years.
